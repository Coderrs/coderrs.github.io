Hyperledger Blockchain Solutions

At the heart of a blockchain network is a distributed ledger that records all the transactions that take place on the network. A blockchain ledger is often described as decentralized because it is replicated across many network participants, each of whom collaborate in its maintenance. We’ll see that decentralization and collaboration are powerful attributes that mirror the way businesses exchange goods and services in the real world.
